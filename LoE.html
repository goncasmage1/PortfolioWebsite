Law Of Eden

Project Description

Law Of Eden is a 3D puzzle platformer created by me and currently in development. I'm working with a 3D artist who is making the assets and textures, while i handle the programming, level design and music. In Law Of Eden, you have to traverse puzzles in a 3D environment, but solve them from a 2D perspective.
