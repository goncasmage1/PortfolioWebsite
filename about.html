About Me

My name is Gonçalo Marques. I'm a Game Developer from Lisbon, Portugal and i've been making games since 2016.

The first game I ever made was a Space Invaders like side-scroller with Python and PyGame called Trivialidades. Shortly after that i decided to start learning Unreal Engine 4 and have since made several projects using it. Near the end of 2016, i also decided to start learning Unity and C#, mostly for 2D purposes.

In November of 2016 i decided to start a project i now call Unreal Gear Solid, an attempt at implementing some of the mechanics seen in the Metal Gear Solid franchise. This project is still ongoing but only sporadically.

Shortly after that, at the start of 2016, i had an idea for a simple game involving a mix of 3D platforming and 2D puzzling, a game that would go on to become Law Of Eden. I thought it would be a good idea to try and take this project to release, given that the mechanics and concept of the game were relatively simple to execute, and that i would learn a lot about some of the intricacies of game development in the process, all the way from concept to finished product, including working closely with people of other disciplines such as 3D art.

I am currently on the third year of my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, and hence can only work on these projects in my free time. Currently i am occasionally available for part-time freelance work as a developer. If you wish to check on the progress on any of my projects, feel free to browse them here, or my GitHub profile for other miscellaneous projects i made/contributed to.
