About Me

Hi! I'm Gonçalo Marques, a Game Developer from Lisbon, Portugal and i've been making games since 2016.

My tools of choice are Unreal Engine 4 and sometimes Unity 5. I love gameplay programming and implementing weird game mechanics.

I am currently on the first year of my Master's Degree in Computer Science and am occasionally available for part-time freelance work as a developer. If you wish to check on the progress on any of my projects, feel free to browse them here, or take a look at my GitHub profile for other miscellaneous projects i made/contributed to.
